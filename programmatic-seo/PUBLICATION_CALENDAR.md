## üí° Pro Tips

### 1. **Batch Content Creation**
- Write 10-15 pieces at once (1-2x/week)
- Store in drafts in Contentful
- Publish progressively every 2 days

### 2. **Quality Over Speed**
- Better to skip a publication day than publish low-quality
- If content isn't ready, wait until next session
- Maintain 1,500+ words per page minimum

### 3. **Monitor and Adapt**
- If indexation is slow (< 70%), slow down publications
- If rankings are good early, can accelerate slightly
- Watch for any duplicate content warnings

### 4. **Optimize Between Publications**
- Use 2-day gaps to analyze data
- Adjust upcoming content based on what's working
- Improve internal linking as site grows
